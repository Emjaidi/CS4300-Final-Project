Here is a little bit about me:

I enjoy spending my evenings reading. Recently, I have been reading a romance novel ( a recommendation from a friend) called 'Book Lovers' by an author named Emily Henry. I usually don't read romance books; I like to think that my taste in literature is eclectic. The book I read before this one was part ghost story, part historical fiction, and part sci-fi! I enjoy fiction books that do not conform to any one genre or trope. I also enjoy reading biographies. I think reading biographies helped me realize that no matter the situation one currently is, that there is always a part of the story not yet lived, and sometimes knowing the story of someone's life helps me live mine because they have lived through their mistakes and in a way, they are teaching me a lesson. One biography that has impacted me is called ‘M Train’ by Patti Smith. She is a poet, musician, and storyteller. After reading her biography it inspired me to write and read more. One other biography I was influenced by was Jaron Lainier’s ‘The Dawn of the New Everything’. In the book, he reflects on his journey through his life from a boy who has lost his mother to a pioneer in the virtual reality industry.
